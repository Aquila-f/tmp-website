I’m a Senior Engineer at **Qualcomm**, working on computer vision and applied AI. I’m interested in the part of AI engineering that begins after a promising prototype: evaluation, latency, reliability, system boundaries, and whether the result remains useful in production.

## Experience

Before Qualcomm, I was a Product Developer on Synology’s Generative AI Application team. I led **AI Advisor**, a customer-facing chatbot serving more than 50,000 users each month, and worked on **Synology InSyde**, an AI agent for multi-step task automation on DSM.

Earlier, I was a Research Assistant at the Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica. My work on CNN–Transformer architectures for board-game reinforcement learning contributed to a publication at **IJCAI-25**.

## Background

I received my M.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from **National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University**, with a GPA of **4.07 / 4.3**.

My path into computer science was not completely linear. That experience shaped how I work today: learn the fundamentals, test assumptions with evidence, and keep improving the model rather than defending the first answer.
