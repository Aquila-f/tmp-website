## Overview

**AI Advisor** is Synology's customer-facing AI chatbot that provides intelligent product recommendations and support to users worldwide. Serving over **50,000 global users per month**, the project was explicitly commended by the CEO to executive leadership for delivering exceptional accuracy and user experience.

## Key Achievements

- **Evaluation Pipeline**: Built a comprehensive evaluation pipeline for response quality and achieved **50%+ improvement** through iterative tuning
- **Performance Optimization**: Cut non-API latency by **30%** through multiprocess, gRPC/REST connection control and caching strategies
- **AI Agents**: Developed NAS/NVR AI agents integrated with internal databases for real-time product recommendation
- **Cross-functional Collaboration**: Worked with Marketing, Design, and MIS teams to align technical deliverables with business objectives

## Technical Highlights

- LLM-based conversational AI system
- Real-time product recommendation engine
- Integration with internal product databases
- Performance-optimized backend architecture
